Nomad is embodying it's technology stack into an enterprise software solution ready to be installed upon any core business solution.

A computing platform designed to convert legacy systems into platform as a service solutions.

Cortex is an application framework, an orchestration manager and a tool collection which runs as a shell around existing  technology structures and turning them into Platform as a Service solutions. Model, build and scale your business application without upgrading legacy parts.

Cortex as a Financial Middleware

  • Monetisation of Data: "Understand"

    Emerging revenue models are broad and tend to function using multiple different types of revenue stream including advertisement and the monetisation of data.
    "Cortex" uses performance marketing methodology combined with Financial KPIs on Big Data collected from all channels and integration points. Real time monitoring and understanding

  • Mobile, Transparent and Instant Financial Services: "Serve"

    "Cortex" creates a secure, high performance and flexible single entry point to financial services of core banking, which replaces "multi channel" term of finance business with "omni channel, user oriented and instant" financial services

  • Infrastructure Replacement: "Sustain"

    Using cutting edge technologies and smart methodologies developed/proven within mobile banking application projects, "Cortex" provides a safe, flexible and controllable staging environment for IT Departments to renew infrastructures in a seamless way.

  • Financial Services Developer Community: "Collaborate"

    Fundamentals of Financial Business is changing, consumers are increasingly aggregating information online from a range of providers about the service they want, the banking industry will face the same disruptive transformation as the media and music industries.
    "Cortex" provides a programmatic developer control layer, giving corporate ITs ability to create an ecosystem for small businesses and entrepreneurs. Similar to today's huge social network providers, breaking the barrier between developers and corporate financial businesses will let innovative and disruptive ideas to be executed.
